If your location experiences temperatures that drop below freezing, you&#39;ll need to take steps to ensure that your pool stays healthy. Also, drain as much water as possible from the filter and heater. Any remaining water can be eliminated using nontoxic antifreeze (caution: this is different from antifreeze for vehicles).
